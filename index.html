Don't miss out on the Mindscape Fest! A place where music, discussions, and activities merge to shine a light on mental health awareness across the world.

Our Mission

We are a group of people dedicated to bring attention about mental health among youth and young adults, which is often ignored or looked down upon.

Facts

22.8% of U.S. adults experienced mental illness in 2021 (57.8 million people). This represents 1 in 5 adults.

1 in 6 U.S. youth aged 6-17 experience a mental health disorder each year.

Our goal is to end stigma, teach about mental health, and offer help. Everyone should feel safe asking for support without feeling scared. Come join us!
