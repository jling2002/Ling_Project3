Ways to GIVE support

Showing you care and offering support

Letting your friend or family member know that help is out there and that mental health issues can be managed

Being open to conversations, listening, and showing interest when mental health topics arise

Providing reassurance and expressing your concern for your friend or family member

Ways to GET support

For support from caring, trained counselors, you can call or text any of the following numbers:

Call or text 988, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to reach the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Call Teen Line at 1-800-852-8336 (6 PM-10 PM PT) or text TEEN to 839863 (6 PM-9 PM PT).
