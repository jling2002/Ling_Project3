Welcome to Mindscape Fest!

Every year, millions of Americans suffer from mental illness. It is important to know how many people have mental illness so we can understand how it affects their bodies, relationships, and money. Furthermore, it serves as a reminder that people are not struggling alone.

Facts

22.8% of U.S. adults experienced mental illness in 2021 (57.8 million people). This represents 1 in 5 adults.

1 in 6 U.S. youth aged 6-17 experience a mental health disorder each year

Our goal is to destigmatize mental health and create a society of openness and support. Join us in this journey!
