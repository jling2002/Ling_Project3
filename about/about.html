CL1MATE

"By combining music and conferences, the CL1MATE festival aims to raise awareness on the different environmental injustice that happens all over the world on May 27 to 29." Purchase your tickets below!

day 2

Listen to the voices of the unheard. Day 2 of CL1MATE consists of two discussion panels from environmental justice speakers all over the world such as Ben Chavis, Winona LaDuke, Amita Baviskar, and many more.

day 3

Every penny counts! The last day of the CL1MATE festival will have two fundraising activities! One of it being DIY handmade paper and the other is planting bee-friendly flower patches!
