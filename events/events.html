Our Event

Day 1 - May 25 (1PM - 6PM): Opening Ceremony & Panel Discussion

The festival will start with musical performances by Ariana Grande, Selena Gomez, and Demi Lovato.


Day 2 - May 26 (10AM - 3PM): Storytelling, Art Exhibition & Workshops

On the second day, there will be storytelling sessions, an art exhibition on mental health in the Asian community, and workshops on stress management, anxiety, and resilience.


Day 3 - May 27 (10AM - 3PM): Yoga, Community Fair & Closing Remarks

The last day begins with a group yoga or meditation session by Ravi Dixit. This will be followed by a community fair with mental health organizations. The event concludes with a summary from the organizers and feedback from attendees.
